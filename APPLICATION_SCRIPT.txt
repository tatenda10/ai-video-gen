AI SHORT-FORM VIDEO GENERATION APPLICATION
Script for Frontend Design - Stitch with Google

================================================================================
APPLICATION OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This is an AI-powered short-form video generation platform that creates 
professional vertical videos (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts style) 
entirely from a text idea. Users input a simple concept, and the system 
generates a complete video with images, voiceover, and talking-head scenes.

================================================================================
CORE FUNCTIONALITY
================================================================================

1. TEXT INPUT TO VIDEO OUTPUT
   - User enters a video idea (e.g., "Promote my AI companion app")
   - User selects tone (warm, professional, energetic, etc.)
   - User chooses platform (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
   - User sets duration (15, 30, 60 seconds)
   - System generates complete video automatically

2. AUTOMATED VIDEO GENERATION PIPELINE
   - Script Generation: AI creates a structured video script with scenes
   - Image Generation: AI creates custom images for each scene
   - Voice Generation: AI generates natural-sounding voiceover
   - Video Generation: AI creates talking-head videos with lip sync
   - Video Assembly: All elements are combined into final video

3. TALKING HEAD FEATURE
   - Creates realistic talking avatar holding a smartphone
   - Avatar speaks with natural lip sync matching the audio
   - Phone screen is visible showing relevant content
   - Professional quality, portrait orientation (9:16 aspect ratio)

================================================================================
USER FLOW
================================================================================

STEP 1: INPUT
   - User lands on homepage
   - Simple form with three fields:
     * Video Idea (text area, placeholder: "Describe your video idea...")
     * Tone (dropdown: Warm, Professional, Energetic, Casual, etc.)
     * Platform (dropdown: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
     * Duration (dropdown: 15s, 30s, 60s)
   - Large "Generate Video" button

STEP 2: PROCESSING
   - After clicking Generate, show progress screen
   - Real-time status updates:
     * "Generating script..." (Step 1/6)
     * "Creating images..." (Step 2/6)
     * "Generating voice..." (Step 3/6)
     * "Creating videos..." (Step 4/6)
     * "Assembling final video..." (Step 5/6)
     * "Complete!" (Step 6/6)
   - Progress bar with percentage
   - Estimated time remaining
   - User can see which step is currently processing

STEP 3: PREVIEW & DOWNLOAD
   - Video preview player (vertical 9:16 format)
   - Play/pause controls
   - Download button (high quality MP4)
   - Share button (copy link, social media)
   - "Generate Another" button to start over
   - Option to regenerate with different settings
